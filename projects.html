01. Self-Driving Cart

// Target: Programming and robotics control.

My first major project involved transforming a standard hoverboard into a functional self-driving cart. By integrating hardware components and sensors such as a digital speedometer to regulate the speed I was able to create a unique vehicle. The project provided hands-on experience in bridging the gap between mechanical parts and programmatic control.

02. AI Smart Bin

// Target: Introduction to AI and proximity detection.

My second project was a self-opening bin designed to automatically open when it detects an object or hand in front of it. I utilized basic AI tools for the detection mechanism, creating this specifically to familiarize myself with artificial intelligence concepts before fully diving into my App & AI studies.
